Stickee uses **passwordless sign-in** powered by Supabase. Instead of creating and remembering a password, you enter your email address and Stickee sends you a one-time magic link. Click the link to sign in — that's it. There's nothing to install for the web version, and no account dashboard to configure before you start taking notes.

## Sign In to Stickee

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Stickee">
    Navigate to the Stickee web app in your browser, or launch the Stickee desktop application on Windows, macOS, or Linux. You'll land on the sign-in screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Email Address">
    Type your email address into the **Email address** field and click **Send magic link**. Stickee contacts Supabase to generate a secure, one-time sign-in link tied to your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Your Inbox">
    Open your email client and look for a message from Stickee. The subject line will reference your sign-in link. Check your spam or junk folder if it doesn't appear within a minute or two.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the Magic Link">
    Click the link inside the email. Your browser (or the desktop app) opens and you're authenticated instantly — no password prompt, no confirmation screen. You'll land directly on your Stickee note board.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Magic links expire after a short time for security. If you click a link and see an error, return to the sign-in screen and request a new one by entering your email again.
</Note>

## Your Profile

Once you're signed in, your display name appears in the top bar of the Stickee interface alongside a user icon. Your display name is generated automatically from your account — no additional profile setup is required. You can see it at a glance whenever you open the app.

## Syncing Across Devices

Sign in with the same email address on any other device — a second computer, a different browser, or the desktop app on another machine — and your complete note board appears automatically. Stickee stores your notes securely in the cloud using Supabase, and every note's title and content is encrypted before it reaches the database. This means:

- **Your data is portable.** Switch devices freely; your notes follow your email address.
- **Your data is private.** Note content and titles are encrypted in the database and cannot be read by anyone else.
- **Your data is durable.** Because notes live in the cloud rather than on a single device, you never lose them if your device is lost or replaced.

<Tip>
  Bookmark the Stickee web app URL in your browser, or install the desktop app, so you can open your note board with a single click. The desktop app built with Tauri is lightweight and launches quickly, making it ideal for always-on capture.
</Tip>
