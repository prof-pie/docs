By the end of this guide you'll have your first note on the board and a personalized workspace — all in under five minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your First Note">
    Once you're on the board, create your first note:

    1. Click the **+** button in the top toolbar, or press **N** on your keyboard.
    2. In the **Add a New Stickee Note** dialog, type your note content in the text area (up to 1,500 characters).
    3. Optionally add a **title** (up to 20 characters).
    4. Click one of the **10 color swatches** to pick a background color for your note.
    5. Select a **status** badge — To-Do, Doing, or Done — if you want to track its progress.
    6. Click **Save Note** (or press **Ctrl+Enter**) to post it to your board.

    Your new note appears on the board with a subtle rotation, just like a real sticky note.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize Your Board">
    With notes on your board, start arranging them to match how you think:

    - **Drag and drop** any note to reorder your board. Your custom order is saved automatically.
    - **Pin** important notes by clicking the pin icon on a note card — pinned notes always float to the top.
    - **Search** for notes using the search bar in the top toolbar. Stickee filters in real time across content, titles, colors, and statuses.
    - **Archive** notes you no longer need front-and-center by clicking the archive icon on a note card. Access them later from the Archive dialog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Your Experience">
    Make Stickee feel like yours:

    1. Click the **Settings** (gear) icon in the toolbar to open Settings.
    2. Under the **UI** tab, choose **Light**, **Dark**, or **System** theme.
    3. Switch to the **Fonts** tab to browse 60+ fonts. Toggle between **Basic Fonts** and **Handwriting** styles, then select the one you want to apply across the app.
    4. Click the ❤️ heart icon next to any font to bookmark it. Bookmarked fonts appear in the **Bookmarks** tab for fast access later.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Press **Alt+P** anywhere to open the **Quick Note** popup — a lightweight overlay for capturing a thought immediately without opening the full note dialog. Press **Ctrl+Enter** or **Escape** to close it and save your content.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Your notes are stored locally in your browser. Use the export/import feature to backup your notes or transfer them between devices.
</Note>
