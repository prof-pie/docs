Stickee's **StickeeList** lets you embed a fully interactive task list directly inside any note. Instead of scattering to-do items across plain-text notes, you get checkboxes, real-time completion tracking, and cloud sync — all without leaving your board.

## Adding a Checklist to a Note

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open or create a note">
    Click an existing note to open it, or press **N** to create a new one on your board.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the checklist panel">
    Click the **List icon** (☰) in the bottom-right corner of the app. The StickeeList panel slides up above the button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start adding tasks">
    Type your first task in the **"Add a new task…"** input field and click **Add** (or press **Enter**). The item appears immediately in the list below.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You must accept the Stickee Terms of Service before you can create or interact with checklists. If you haven't agreed yet, the checklist button will appear greyed out and disabled until you accept.
</Note>

## Managing Checklist Items

Once your checklist is open, you have full control over every item:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Add a new item">
    Type the task text in the input at the top of the panel and click **Add** or press **Enter**. Stickee saves the item to the cloud the moment you add it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Toggle an item complete or incomplete">
    Click the **checkbox** to the left of any item. A check mark appears and the item text dims with a strikethrough to show it's done. Click again to uncheck it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Edit an existing item">
    Click directly on the item's text to enter edit mode. Make your changes, then press **Enter** to save or **Escape** to cancel. Clicking away from the item also saves automatically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Delete an item">
    Click the **trash icon** (🗑) on the right side of any item to remove it permanently. The deletion syncs to your cloud account immediately.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

All changes — adding, toggling, editing, and deleting — are persisted to your Stickee cloud account in real time. You never need to press a manual save button.

## Checklist Tips

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Everyday lists" icon="cart-shopping">
    Use StickeeList for grocery runs, packing checklists, or errand lists. Check items off as you go.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Project tracking" icon="list-check">
    Break a project into steps, assign one item per action, and work through them sequentially.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Meeting agendas" icon="calendar-check">
    List agenda points before a meeting, then check them off in real time as you cover each one.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Pair checklists with note statuses for extra clarity:**

- Set the related note's status to **Doing** while you're actively working through the list.
- Flip it to **Done** once every item is checked.
- **Pin** the note so your active checklist always sits at the top of your board.

<Tip>
  Your checklist items sync to the cloud just like notes — open Stickee on any device and your tasks are right where you left them.
</Tip>
