**Quick Note** is a fast-capture popup that lets you jot down an idea the moment it strikes — no navigating to your board, no searching for an empty note, no interruption to whatever you were doing. Press a keyboard shortcut, type, and save. That's it.

## Opening Quick Note

<Steps>
  <Step title='Press Alt+P'>
    Press **Alt+P** anywhere inside the Stickee app (web or desktop) to open the Quick Note popup instantly. A focused modal appears over your current screen with the cursor already in the text area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type your note">
    Write your thought, idea, or reminder. Quick Note accepts up to **1,500 characters** — enough for a detailed note but short enough to keep the capture fast. A live character counter at the bottom of the popup shows how many characters you've used (e.g., *42/1500 characters*).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Press **Ctrl+Enter** (Windows/Linux) or **Cmd+Enter** (Mac) to save the note, or click the **close (✕) button** in the top-right corner. Stickee adds it as a new sticky note on your main board.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Close and save">
    Press **Escape** to close the popup. Just like **Ctrl+Enter** / **Cmd+Enter**, this saves your note — it is added to your board with whatever content you've typed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How Quick Note Works

When you save a Quick Note, Stickee:

- Creates a **new sticky note** on your main board with the content you typed.
- Applies your **default color** and sets the status to **To-Do** automatically.
- Places the note on your board so you can find, edit, or organize it whenever you're ready.

You don't need to worry about color, title, or placement while capturing — those details can wait until later.

<Tip>
  Use Quick Note whenever an idea strikes mid-task. Don't stop to organize it — just capture it and keep going. You can always come back to edit the content, change the color, assign a status, or archive it when you have time.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Quick Note is especially powerful in the **Stickee desktop app** (built with Tauri). Alt+P is available whenever the Stickee window is in focus, making it the fastest possible path from thought to saved note without reaching for the mouse.
</Note>
