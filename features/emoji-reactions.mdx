**Emoji reactions** let you add expressive visual markers to any note on your board. At a glance you can see which notes are urgent, which are favorites, and which belong to a certain topic — all without opening a single note.

## Adding a Reaction

<Steps>
  <Step title="Hover over a note card">
    Move your cursor over any note on your board to reveal the note's action buttons, including the **emoji (😊) button**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the emoji picker">
    Click the emoji button to open the emoji picker panel. It shows the six available reactions you can add.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select an emoji">
    Click any emoji in the picker to add it as a reaction to that note. The picker closes automatically and your reaction appears on the note card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See the reaction count">
    The emoji badge on the note card shows a count. The count increases each time a reaction is added and decreases when one is removed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Toggling Reactions

You don't need to open the emoji picker to remove a reaction you've already added:

- Click an **existing reaction badge** directly on the note card to toggle it.
- If you've already reacted with that emoji, clicking it **removes** your reaction.
- If you haven't reacted yet, clicking it **adds** your reaction.
- The count updates in real time after every toggle.

## Available Reactions

Stickee provides a curated set of six reactions to keep your board clean and expressive:

| Emoji | Common use |
|-------|------------|
| ❤️ | Favorites, love, personal notes |
| 👍 | Approved, agreed, looks good |
| 😊 | Positive vibes, feel-good notes |
| 🎉 | Celebrations, milestones, wins |
| 😂 | Funny, lighthearted content |
| *(Stickee logo)* | Anything Stickee-specific — your personal stamp |

## Why Use Reactions

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Flag urgent items" icon="circle-exclamation">
    Drop a ❤️ or 👍 on a note to mark it as high-priority so it stands out the moment you open your board.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mark favorites" icon="star">
    React with ❤️ to build a visual collection of your most important or beloved notes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Add mood context" icon="face-smile">
    Use 😊 or 🎉 to give a note an emotional tone — useful for journaling or celebrating completed goals.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Categorize by topic" icon="tags">
    Assign consistent emoji to recurring categories (e.g., 🎉 for project milestones) so you can spot them instantly on a busy board.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Reactions are purely visual — they don't affect search results or note statuses. Think of them as sticky labels you slap on a physical note: they add meaning at a glance without changing the underlying content.
</Tip>
