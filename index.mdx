Welcome to **Stickee** — your digital sticky note board that goes everywhere you do. Stickee lets you capture ideas, track tasks, and organize your thoughts with colorful, cloud-synced notes accessible from any web browser or directly from your desktop. With end-to-end encryption keeping your content private, 10 vibrant note colors, 60+ font choices, voice input, built-in checklists, and real-time sync across all your devices, Stickee brings the satisfying simplicity of a physical sticky note wall into the modern age.

## Get Up and Running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Open Stickee in your browser or desktop app and enter your email address. Stickee sends you a magic link — no password required. Click the link in your inbox to sign in instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your First Note">
    Click the **+** button in the toolbar (or press **N** anywhere on your board) to open the new note dialog. Type your content, optionally add a title, choose a color, and click **Save Note**. Your note appears on the board immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Your Experience">
    Open **Settings** to switch between light and dark themes, browse more than 60 fonts (including handwriting styles), and bookmark your favorites for quick access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sync Everywhere">
    Sign in with the same email on any other device — web or desktop — and all your notes appear automatically. Stickee encrypts your note content and titles before storing them, so your data stays private.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Press **N** anywhere on your board to open the new note dialog instantly. For an even faster capture experience, press **Alt+P** to pop open the Quick Note overlay without leaving whatever you're working on.
</Tip>
