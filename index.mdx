Welcome to **Stickee** — your digital sticky note board. Stickee lets you capture ideas, track tasks, and organize your thoughts with colorful notes accessible from any web browser or directly from your desktop. With 10 vibrant note colors, 60+ font choices, voice input, built-in checklists, and local storage, Stickee brings the satisfying simplicity of a physical sticky note wall into the modern age.

## Get Up and Running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Stickee">
    Open Stickee in your browser or launch the desktop app. You'll see your note board ready to use.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your First Note">
    Click the **+** button in the toolbar (or press **N** anywhere on your board) to open the new note dialog. Type your content, optionally add a title, choose a color, and click **Save Note**. Your note appears on the board immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Your Experience">
    Open **Settings** to switch between light and dark themes, browse more than 60 fonts (including handwriting styles), and bookmark your favorites for quick access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Backup Your Notes">
    Use the export feature to backup your notes or transfer them between devices. Your notes are stored locally in your browser's storage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Press **N** anywhere on your board to open the new note dialog instantly. For an even faster capture experience, press **Alt+P** to pop open the Quick Note overlay without leaving whatever you're working on.
</Tip>
