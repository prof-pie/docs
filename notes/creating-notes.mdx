Stickee gives you a full set of tools to create, customize, and manage your sticky notes. You can give each note a title, pick from ten colors, assign a status to track progress, and edit or delete notes at any time — all from one focused interface.

## Create a New Note

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Add Note dialog">
    Click the **+ Add Note** button, or press <kbd>N</kbd> on your keyboard to open the new-note dialog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your content">
    Type your note text in the **Content** field. Content is required — the Save button stays disabled until you add something. You can write up to **1,500 characters**. Press <kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>Enter</kbd> (or <kbd>⌘</kbd>+<kbd>Enter</kbd> on macOS) to save without reaching for the mouse.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add an optional title">
    Type a short label in the **Title** field (up to 20 characters). Titles appear bold at the top of the note card on your board.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a color">
    Select one of the ten color swatches: **yellow**, **pink**, **blue**, **green**, **purple**, **orange**, **teal**, **lavender**, **peach**, or **mint**. The selected swatch is highlighted with a border and scaled up slightly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a status">
    Click a status badge — **To-Do**, **Doing**, or **Done** — to set the note's initial status. The default is **To-Do**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save Note**. Your new note appears on the board immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Edit a Note

Click any note card on your board to open it in the detail view. From there you can update:

- The **title** and **content**
- The **color**
- The **status**

When you're done making changes, click **Save Changes** to save. If you close the dialog without saving, Stickee will ask whether you want to save or discard your edits.

## Delete a Note

<Warning>
  Deleting a note permanently removes it. This action cannot be undone.
</Warning>

1. Click the note you want to delete to open its detail dialog.
2. Select the **Delete** option inside the dialog.
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

<Tip>
  Consider using **Archive** instead of Delete if there's any chance you'll want the note later. Archived notes are hidden from your board but remain accessible in the Archive view.
</Tip>

## Note Attributes

Every note in Stickee is made up of the following fields:

<ParamField path="title" type="string">
  An optional short label displayed at the top of the note card. Maximum **20 characters**.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="content" type="string" required>
  The main body text of the note. This field is required. Maximum **1,500 characters**.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="color" type="string">
  The background color of the note card. Choose from ten options: `yellow`, `pink`, `blue`, `green`, `purple`, `orange`, `teal`, `lavender`, `peach`, or `mint`.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="status" type="string">
  The current progress state of the note. One of `To-Do`, `Doing`, or `Done`. Defaults to `To-Do`.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="pinned" type="boolean">
  When `true`, the note is pinned and always appears at the top of your board, regardless of sort order.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="archived" type="boolean">
  When `true`, the note is hidden from the main board and moved to the Archive. Archived notes are not deleted.
</ParamField>

<Note>
  Your notes are stored securely. Only you can access them.
</Note>
