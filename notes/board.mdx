The **Stickee board** is your main workspace — the digital wall where all your sticky notes live, move, and interact. Think of it as your personal command center for ideas, tasks, and projects. Every note you create appears here, arranged in the order you choose, with the colors, statuses, and customizations that match your workflow.

## Board Overview

When you open Stickee, you'll see your board immediately. Here's what makes up the interface:

## Board Views

Stickee gives you two ways to view your notes, depending on how you prefer to work:

| View | Description | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Grid View** | Notes appear as colorful cards arranged in a masonry-style grid with natural rotation | Visual organization, color-coding, spatial thinking |
| **List View** | Notes appear in a compact vertical list with clear titles and statuses | Reading titles at a glance, quickly scanning many notes |

Switch between views using the view toggle in the toolbar. Your preference is saved automatically.

## Board Organization

Your board is highly customizable. Here are the key ways to organize it:

### Drag and Drop
Click and drag any note to move it to a new position. Stickee saves your custom order automatically, so your arrangement persists across sessions.

**Note:** Pinned notes always stay at the top regardless of drag position. Drag to reorder only affects unpinned notes.

### Pinning
Pin important notes to lock them at the top of your board. Pinned notes:
- Always appear above all unpinned notes
- Maintain their own order among themselves
- Are perfect for daily reference items (meeting agendas, recurring checklists, important links)

### Status Sorting
Use note statuses (**To-Do**, **Doing**, **Done**) to create a lightweight task tracking system. Search by status to filter your board to specific workflow stages.

### Color Coding
Assign one of 10 vibrant colors to each note. Use colors to:
- Distinguish project categories
- Mark priority levels
- Create visual patterns that make sense to you
- Make your board more scannable at a glance

## Board Features

Your board includes several features to make it more powerful:

### Real-Time Search
The search bar filters your board instantly as you type. Search across:
- Note content
- Note titles
- Colors
- Statuses
- Emoji reactions

Results update with every keystroke. Clear the search bar to restore your full board.

### Quick Actions
Right-click on any note card to access quick actions:
- **Edit** — Open the note in detail view
- **Pin/Unpin** — Toggle pin status
- **Archive** — Move to archive
- **Delete** — Permanently remove

### Keyboard Navigation
Press `N` anywhere on the board to create a new note instantly. Use `Ctrl+Enter` to save notes quickly. See the [Keyboard Shortcuts](/settings/keyboard-shortcuts) docs for more.

## Board Best Practices

<Tip>
  Pin the notes you reference daily — standing meeting agendas, recurring checklists, or important links. They'll always be waiting at the top when you open Stickee.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  At the end of each week, archive all your **Done** notes. Your board resets to only active work, and your archive becomes a log of everything you accomplished.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  Use color coding consistently across projects. For example, use blue for work tasks, green for personal items, and yellow for ideas. This makes your board instantly scannable.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Your board is stored locally in your browser's storage. Use the export/import feature to backup your board arrangement or transfer it between devices.
</Note>

## Board vs. Archive

Your board shows active, current notes. The **Archive** is where completed or infrequently needed notes live safely out of sight. Think of it this way:

| | Board | Archive |
|---|---|---|
| **Purpose** | Active work and current reference | Completed work and storage |
| **Visibility** | Always visible | Hidden until you open Archive view |
| **Search** | Searched by default | Searched when Archive is open |
| **Pin Status** | Pinning works here | Pinned notes return to board when unarchived |

Move notes between board and archive freely — nothing is ever deleted unless you choose to delete it.

<Info>
  The board is your living workspace. Keep it focused on what matters right now, and use the archive as a clean storage area for everything else.
</Info>
