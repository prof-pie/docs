Stickee gives you several ways to keep your board clean and navigable. You can drag notes into any order, switch between grid and list views, pin the notes you reference most, and filter your board instantly with search. Use these tools together to build a board that works exactly the way you think.

## Drag and Drop

You can reorder notes freely by dragging any note card to a new position on the board. Stickee saves the new order automatically — the next time you open the app your arrangement is preserved exactly as you left it.

<Note>
  Pinned notes always stay at the top of the board regardless of the drag order. Reordering only affects unpinned notes.
</Note>

## Grid and List Views

Use the **view toggle** in the toolbar to switch between two display modes:

| View | Best for |
|------|----------|
| **Grid** | A visual overview — notes appear as cards arranged in a masonry-style grid, great for color-coded boards and spatial thinking. |
| **List** | Dense information — notes appear as compact rows, great when you have many notes and want to scan them quickly. |

<Tip>
  Switch to **List view** when your board gets crowded. You can read note titles and statuses at a glance without scrolling as much.
</Tip>

## Pinned Notes

Pinned notes always appear at the very top of your board, above all other notes, regardless of sort order or drag position. Use pinning for notes you open every day — standing meeting agendas, recurring checklists, or important links.

See [Pinning & Archiving](/notes/pinning-archiving) for full details on how to pin and unpin notes.

## Search and Filter

The **search bar** filters your board in real time as you type. You can search by:

- Note **content**
- Note **title**
- **Color** name (e.g., `blue` or `mint`)
- **Status** name (e.g., `doing` or `done`)

The board updates instantly with every keystroke, and the full note list is restored the moment you clear the search field. See the Search docs for advanced filtering details.
