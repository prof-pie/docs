Stickee gives you two tools for keeping your board manageable without losing anything important. **Pinning** promotes your most-used notes to a permanent spot at the very top of the board. **Archiving** tucks completed or infrequently needed notes out of sight while keeping them safely stored and searchable. Use them together to maintain a board that only shows what's relevant right now.

## Pinning Notes

Pin a note to lock it at the top of your board so it's always the first thing you see when you open Stickee.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pin a note">
    Click the **pin icon** in the top-right corner of any note card. The icon turns red to confirm the note is pinned.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View pinned notes">
    All pinned notes move to the top of the board immediately, above every unpinned note. Their position is preserved across sessions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unpin a note">
    Click the **pin icon** again to unpin. The note returns to the regular board order.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You can pin as many notes as you like. Pinned notes are also sorted among themselves, so you can drag them into a preferred order at the top of the board.
</Note>

## Archiving Notes

Archive a note to remove it from your main board without deleting it. Archived notes are stored safely and can be restored at any time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Archive a note">
    Click the **archive icon** on the note card (to the left of the pin icon). The note disappears from your main board immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access archived notes">
    Open the **Archive** from the navigation menu or toolbar. Your archived notes appear here, sorted by most recently archived first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restore a note">
    Click **Unarchive** on any archived note to move it back to the main board, exactly as it was.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Permanently delete an archived note">
    From the Archive view, select the note and choose **Delete**. This permanently removes the note and cannot be undone.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Permanently deleting a note from the Archive is irreversible. If you're unsure, leave the note archived — you can always come back to it later.
</Warning>

## When to Use Each

| Situation | Use |
|-----------|-----|
| Standing meeting agenda you open every day | **Pin** |
| Recurring checklist or important link | **Pin** |
| A completed project you might reference later | **Archive** |
| Old notes that are cluttering your board | **Archive** |
| Notes you're certain you no longer need | **Delete** (from Archive) |

<Tip>
  A good habit: at the end of each week, archive all your **Done** notes. Your board resets to only active work, and your archive becomes a log of everything you accomplished.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Archived notes still count toward your note storage and remain securely stored — they are accessible only to you, even from the archive.
</Note>
