Stickee supports both light and dark themes so your workspace always feels comfortable, whether you're working in a bright office or winding down for the evening. You can switch themes in seconds, and Stickee even keeps in sync with your operating system automatically.

## Switching Themes

You have two ways to change your theme:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Theme Toggle Icon">
    Click the **paintbrush icon** (🎨) in the top navigation bar to open a small popover menu. From there, select **Light**, **Dark**, or **System** — the change applies instantly, no reload required.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Settings Panel">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Settings">
        Click the **gear icon** in the top navigation bar to open the Settings dialog.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Go to the UI tab">
        Select the **UI** tab at the top of the Settings dialog.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose your theme">
        Under the **Theme** section, select one of the three radio options: **Light**, **Dark**, or **System**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## System Theme

When you set your theme to **System**, Stickee reads your operating system's appearance preference and applies the matching theme automatically. If you switch your OS from light to dark mode (or vice versa), Stickee updates in real time — no manual changes needed.

<Info>
  **System** is the recommended default. It keeps Stickee visually consistent with the rest of your desktop environment without any extra effort on your part.
</Info>

This works on all supported platforms:

| Platform | Where to set OS appearance |
|---|---|
| **macOS** | System Settings → Appearance |
| **Windows** | Settings → Personalization → Colors |
| **Linux** | Varies by desktop environment (e.g., GNOME Settings → Appearance) |

<Tip>
  The dark theme is especially pleasant for evening note-taking sessions — the warm note colors pop beautifully against the dark background, making your board feel inviting rather than harsh on the eyes.
</Tip>
