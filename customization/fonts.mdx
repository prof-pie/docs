Stickee offers 60+ fonts to make your board feel truly personal. Whether you prefer a clean professional typeface, a techy monospace, or an expressive hand-drawn style, there's a font that fits your workflow and personality. Your chosen font applies across all your notes instantly and syncs with your account automatically.

## Changing Your Font

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click the **gear icon** in the top navigation bar to open the Settings dialog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Fonts tab">
    Select the **Fonts** tab at the top of the Settings dialog. You'll see a banner showing your currently active font.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a font mode">
    Select either **Basic Fonts** or **Handwriting** from the radio group to browse the relevant category.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and select a font">
    Each font name is rendered in its own typeface so you can preview it at a glance. Click any font's radio button to apply it immediately — your entire board updates in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save favorites (optional)">
    Click the **heart icon** next to any font to bookmark it for quick access later. You can save up to 10 favorites.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your font choice is saved automatically to your Stickee account so it persists across sessions and devices.
</Note>

## Favorite Fonts

You can bookmark up to **10 fonts** for instant access — no more scrolling through the full list every time you want to switch.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Heart a font">
    In the **Fonts** tab, click the **heart icon** (♡) to the right of any font name. The heart fills in red to confirm it's saved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access your favorites">
    Switch to the **Bookmarks** tab in Settings to see all your saved fonts in one place.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply from Bookmarks">
    Click **Apply** next to any bookmarked font to switch to it immediately, or click the filled heart to remove it from your favorites.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  You can save a maximum of 10 favorite fonts. Once you reach the limit, click the heart on an existing favorite to remove it before adding a new one.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  Handwriting fonts like **Indie Flower** or **Permanent Marker** give your digital board the tactile feel of real sticky notes — a great choice if you want your workspace to feel warm and personal rather than clinical.
</Tip>
