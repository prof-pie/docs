Every note you create in Stickee is stored locally in your browser's storage. Your note titles and content are stored directly on your device — meaning the raw text of your thoughts is never sent to any external server.

<Note>
  Your notes are stored locally in your browser's storage. There is nothing you need to configure or enable — your notes are always accessible only to you.
</Note>

## What Is Stored Locally

Stickee stores the following fields for every note in your browser's local storage:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Note Content" icon="database">
    The body of every note is stored locally on your device. Your content never leaves your browser unless you choose to export it.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Note Titles" icon="database">
    Optional note titles are stored locally along with your content — your titles never leave your device.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Both fields are stored locally in your browser. When you open a note, Stickee loads the content automatically so you can read it.

## Note Metadata

Some metadata fields are stored along with your notes to power sorting, filtering, and display features:

| Field | Storage |
|---|---|
| Note color | Local storage |
| Note status (To-Do, Doing, Done, Backlog) | Local storage |
| Pinned state | Local storage |
| Archived state | Local storage |
| Timestamps (`created_at`, `updated_at`) | Local storage |
| Checklist item text | Local storage |
| Emoji reactions | Local storage |

<Info>
  All note data, including metadata, is stored locally in your browser. Your notes never leave your device unless you choose to export them.
</Info>

## Why This Matters

<Accordion title="Your data stays on your device">
  Your notes are stored locally in your browser's storage. This means your content never leaves your device unless you choose to export it. Your private thoughts stay private.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="No server dependency">
  Since your notes are stored locally, you don't need to worry about server downtime, database breaches, or external service availability. Your notes are always accessible as long as you have access to your device.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Easy backup and transfer">
  Use the export feature to create backups of your notes or transfer them between devices. You have full control over your data at all times.
</Accordion>
